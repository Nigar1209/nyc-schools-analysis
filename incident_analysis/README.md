### Overview:
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Total records: 6310
- Unique schools: 1891
- Most frequent incident type: Non-Criminal (11772)
- % of Incidents occurred in the Bronx: 28.24%

### Key Insights:
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1) **Consolidated Reporting:** Incidents are often aggregated by building rather than individual school, masking specific safety data for up to 11 schools at a single address.

2) **Non-Criminal Focus:** Over **57% of incidents are "Non-Criminal,"** showing that staff primarily manage behavioral, medical, or social issues rather than actual crimes.

3) **Geographic Clustering:** A small number of high-density campus buildings, largely in the Bronx, account for a disproportionate volume of the city's total reported activity.
